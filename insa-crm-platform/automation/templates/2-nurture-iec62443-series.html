What is IEC 62443?

IEC 62443 is a comprehensive set of standards designed to address cybersecurity for industrial automation and control systems (IACS). It provides a flexible framework applicable to all industrial sectors.

The Four Pillars of IEC 62443

  • General: Concepts, models, and terminology
  • Policies & Procedures: Management system requirements
  • System: Security requirements for control systems
  • Component: Requirements for individual products
