Company Overview

Founded in November 2022, INSA Automation Corp specializes in delivering comprehensive remote design solutions, industrial automation, cybersecurity, and AI-powered security systems. As a pioneer in industrial cybersecurity, we protect critical infrastructure with our innovative AI solutions including the RTA (Red Team Agent) platform. Leveraging the expertise of our sister company, Insa Ingeniería SAS, established in 2010, we bring a rich legacy of optimized processes, enhanced efficiency, security, and sustainable solutions across various sectors, including Oil & Gas, Water & Wastewater, Food & Beverage, Energy, and Critical Infrastructure.
